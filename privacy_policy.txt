All user credentials are encrypted and stored securely on the user's device. We use industry-standard encryption techniques to ensure the protection of this information.
3. Third-Party Sharing
NED Vendor Companion does not share, sell, or transfer any user information to third parties under any circumstances.
4. Data Security
We are committed to protecting your data and take appropriate security measures to safeguard your information. However, users are responsible for ensuring that their devices are secure.
5. User Control
Users can delete or modify their stored credentials directly through the application.
6. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version of the policy within the application.
